ABOUT ME

I am a student at the Faculty of Computer Science & Engineering (FINKI) in Skopje and I am now finishing my first year. Before becoming a student at FINKI, I studied mathematics at the high school Jane Sandanski in Strumica. This is a picture from my yearbook from last year.
